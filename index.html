Who we are

About us

Established in 2014, Leeks Holding and Construction LLC has grown into a leading force in the construction industry, delivering world-class engineering and construction solutions across a wide spectrum of sectors. With a decade-long commitment to excellence, we specialize in the design and construction of high-rise buildings and offshore oil rigs, bringing innovation, safety, and durability to every project.

Verified by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) and federally recognized by the FDIC, Leeks Holding and Construction LLC operates with full financial transparency and regulatory compliance—giving our partners and clients absolute confidence in our integrity, credibility, and long-term stability.

Our team is composed of seasoned engineers, architects, and project managers dedicated to delivering complex builds on time and to the highest standards. Whether shaping skylines or powering offshore exploration, Leeks Holding and Construction LLC stands as a symbol of structural integrity and engineering excellence.
